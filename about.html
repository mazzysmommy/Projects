Get to know us!

Founded in 2023 by childhood friends Mia and Grayson, Bean Haven Café started as a dream to bring world-class coffee and real community connection to our neighborhood. We bring people and cats together to promote less strays on the streets. Half of all of our proceeds go to local rescues to help cats get adopted.

Our Promise

  • Ethically sourced, direct-trade coffee
  • Fresh, handmade pastries every morning
  • Locally and ethically sourced ingredients.
  • A warm space for work, friends, and creativity
  • A relaxing environment for the people and fun place for your cat to socialize.
